"We may encounter many defeats, but we must not be defeated."
— Maya Angelou

Challenges aren't setbacks; they're stepping stones to success—hidden opportunities that shape us. Every obstacle teaches a lesson, and every setback fuels determination. Resilience is about facing difficulties with confidence, learning from each experience, and moving forward with determination. Stay strong, and success will follow those who never give up.
